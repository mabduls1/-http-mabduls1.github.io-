My Hobbies

  • The purpose of this site is to list the different hobbies I have. I have wanted to build something like this in the past but haven't done it yet.
  • I want to accomplish this by putting all of my hobbies in one place. This website would let me share my interests and project with others.
  • The opportunity for creating my site would be having it available and ready to go when connecting with others with the same interest. It is not addressing a big issue for me at the moment.
  • The audience I want my site to attract is people with similar hobbies as myself. It is basically for people who enjoy the same as me. It doesn't matter about age, race, or gender.
  • I would have information on some of the projects I have done. Each page of the site will give a brief description of how I got interested in each hobby.
